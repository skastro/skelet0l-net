◈ ABOUT

Tony Scoburgh

Musician, strategist, planner, builder. I make work at the overlap of systems, music, and human coordination, and I publish it under the name skele.t0l.

What I do

I build the process layer that keeps complex things from collapsing under their own weight. That shows up differently depending on the surface: integrated planning at Procter & Gamble, signals intelligence in the Army, ensemble direction and music performance, or the frameworks and tools I design for my own creative work.

The through-line is the same: read an opaque situation quickly, find the structure inside it, and hand it back in a form people can move on.

What I'm building now

The public body of work is growing. The portfolio site you're on is one piece. The Astral Compendium is the larger creative system underneath it. I'm also studying toward organizational psychology, with a research direction in systems that keep people effective without making them disposable.

What I'm looking for

Work and collaboration that use the full range: strategy, operations, music, communication, systems design. If you need someone who bridges analysis and art, process and feeling, structure and experimentation, that's the lane.
