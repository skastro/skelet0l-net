About

Musician, analyst, planner, and builder. I’ve been fortunate to move through very different worlds, and each one shaped how I think.

I studied oboe performance and languages at the University of Cincinnati College-Conservatory of Music. After graduating in 2018, I enlisted in the Army and served on Active Duty through 2023, then transitioned to the Reserves until 2025. I’m currently a Site Integrated Planner at Procter & Gamble in Personal Health Care.

The thread through all of it is systems under pressure: how organizations communicate, how plans survive contact with reality, and how to build things that hold together when the conditions change. I care about psychological safety, clear thinking, and making useful things with the same attention whether anyone is watching or not.
